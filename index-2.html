ABOUT

YOUR PLYMOUTH HANDYMAN.


Hi I'm Tom. I started my handyman business back in 2019 through a love of DIY and all things home-improvement. After leaving my old job as a BT Systems Support Manager I was nervous about starting a business, but it's the best decision I've ever made and I couldn't be happier in my job!


Every day I am presented with something and someone new, and I had no idea of the enjoyment I would get from meeting so many lovely customers and helping them turn their house into a home.
