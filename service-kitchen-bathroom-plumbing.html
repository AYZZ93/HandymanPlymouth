SERVICE

KITCHEN, BATHROOM & PLUMBING.

I offer a range of services to help you with all your home improvement needs. I take pride in delivering high-quality workmanship and excellent customer service.


Below are some of the services I offer:


  • Bath Sealant and Bath & Shower Silicone


    If your bath or shower sealant is damaged or deteriorating, it can lead to leaks and water damage. I can help you remove and replace old sealant with a new one, ensuring your bathroom remains dry and protected.


  • Grout Replacement


    Over time, grout in between tiles can become stained or cracked, leading to an unsightly appearance. I can help you remove and replace old grout with a new one, ensuring your tiles look fresh and clean.


  • Shower Screens and Riser Rails


    Shower screens and riser rails can become loose or damaged over time, leading to difficulty in opening and closing or even causing an unsafe environment. I can help you repair or replace your shower screens and riser rails, ensuring they are functioning properly and safely.


  • Replacement Bathroom/Kitchen Taps and Sink Wastes


    Leaking or faulty taps and sink wastes can lead to water damage and higher water bills. I can help you replace your old taps and sink wastes with new ones, ensuring they are functioning properly and saving you money in the long run.


  • Washing Machines installation


    If you need a new washing machine installed, I can help you with the installation process. I can also help you with the removal of your old washing machine and ensure the new one is installed to a high standard.


If you require any of the above services or have any other handyman needs, please don't hesitate to contact me. I am always happy to help.
