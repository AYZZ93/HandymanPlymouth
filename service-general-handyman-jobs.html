SERVICE

GENERAL HANDYMAN JOBS.

I offer a range of services to help you with all your home improvement needs. I take pride in delivering high-quality workmanship and excellent customer service.


Below are some of the services I offer:


  • Curtain Poles and Curtain Tracks


    Whether you need new curtain poles or tracks installed or need them repaired, I can help you. I have the necessary tools and experience to get the job done quickly and efficiently.


  • T.V Brackets


    I can install your T.V brackets, ensuring your television is securely mounted and positioned correctly.


  • Flat Pack


    Assembling flat-pack furniture can be a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. I can help you assemble your flat-pack furniture quickly and efficiently, ensuring that it is built to a high standard.


  • Key Safe


    A key safe is a great way to keep your keys secure and easily accessible. I can install your key safe to ensure it is fitted securely and in a convenient location.


  • Mirror Mounting


    Whether you want to mount a mirror in your bathroom or a decorative mirror in your living room, I can help you. I will ensure that your mirror is securely mounted and levelled correctly.


  • Shelves


    From floating shelves to bookshelves, I can help you with all your shelf installation needs. I will work with you to determine the best location and design for your shelves to meet your requirements.


  • Picture Frames Wall Mount


    I can help you mount your picture frames to create a beautiful wall display. I will ensure that the frames are levelled correctly and securely fixed to the wall.


If you require any of the above services or have any other handyman needs, please don't hesitate to contact me. I am always happy to help.
